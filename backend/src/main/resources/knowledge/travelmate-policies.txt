TOPIC: What is TravelMate?
TravelMate is a platform that connects travelers with verified local tour guides around the world. Travelers can discover unique trips, browse experienced guides, send booking requests, and communicate in real-time through built-in chat. Guides can create trip listings, manage their bookings, and build their reputation through traveler reviews. TravelMate makes travel more personal, authentic, and safe by connecting you directly with locals who know their region best.
---
TOPIC: How do I create an account on TravelMate?
To create an account, click "Sign Up" on the homepage. Enter your first name, last name, email address, and create a password. After signing up, you will receive a welcome email confirming your registration. You can then log in and set up your profile as either a Traveler or a Guide.
---
TOPIC: What is the difference between a Traveler and a Guide?
A Traveler is someone looking to explore new destinations with the help of a local guide. A Guide is a local expert who offers trip experiences, creates trip posts, manages bookings, and provides personalized tours. When you set up your profile, you choose your role. Guides need to complete additional profile information including their expertise, languages spoken, and a profile description.
---
TOPIC: How do I set up my profile?
After signing in, go to the Dashboard and click "Complete Profile." If you are a Traveler, you will fill out your travel preferences and interests. If you are a Guide, you will provide details about your location, expertise areas, languages spoken, experience, and a bio. You can also upload a profile photo via Cloudinary. A complete profile helps you get more bookings and build trust.
---
TOPIC: How do I book a guide?
Browse available guides on the Dashboard or explore trip posts on the Open Trips board. Click on a guide's profile to see their details, reviews, and trip offerings. Click "Book Now" to send a booking request. You will need to provide your preferred travel dates, group size, and any special requirements. The guide will review your request and accept or decline it.
---
TOPIC: What is the Open Trips Board?
The Open Trips Board is where guides post available trip experiences that any traveler can browse and join. Each trip post includes the destination, description, duration, price, and the guide's profile. Travelers can click "View Details" to learn more about a trip and then contact the guide or send a booking request directly.
---
TOPIC: How do I create a trip post as a Guide?
As a Guide, go to your Dashboard and click "Create Trip Post." Fill in the trip title, destination, detailed description, duration, price per person, and upload relevant photos. You can use TravelMate's AI-powered text enhancement to improve your trip description. Once published, your trip will appear on the Open Trips Board for travelers to discover.
---
TOPIC: Cancellation and Refund Policy
Travelers can cancel a confirmed booking up to 48 hours before the scheduled trip start date for a full refund. Cancellations made between 24 and 48 hours before the trip will receive a 50% refund. Cancellations made less than 24 hours before the trip or no-shows are not eligible for any refund. To cancel a booking, go to "My Bookings" in your dashboard, find the booking, and click "Cancel Booking." Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.
---
TOPIC: How does payment work on TravelMate?
TravelMate uses secure payment processing for all bookings. When a traveler books a trip, the payment is held securely until the trip is completed. Guides receive their payment within 24 hours after the trip ends and is marked as completed. TravelMate does not store any credit card information directly. All transactions are encrypted and processed through secure payment gateways.
---
TOPIC: How does the chat and messaging system work?
TravelMate has a built-in real-time chat system powered by WebSockets. Once you have a booking or connection with a guide/traveler, you can message them directly from the Dashboard. Messages are delivered instantly and stored securely. You can discuss trip details, ask questions, share meeting points, and coordinate logistics through the chat. Chat is available between connected travelers and guides only.
---
TOPIC: How are guides verified on TravelMate?
All guides on TravelMate go through a verification process. Guides must provide a valid government-issued ID, complete their profile with accurate information, and agree to TravelMate's community guidelines and code of conduct. TravelMate reviews guide profiles for authenticity. Verified guides display a verification badge on their profile. Travelers are encouraged to check reviews and ratings before booking.
---
TOPIC: How do reviews and ratings work?
After completing a trip, travelers can leave a review and rating (1-5 stars) for their guide. Reviews help other travelers make informed decisions and help guides build their reputation. Reviews must be honest, respectful, and based on the actual trip experience. TravelMate reserves the right to remove reviews that contain inappropriate content, spam, or violate community guidelines. Guides can respond to reviews through their profile.
---
TOPIC: What safety tips does TravelMate recommend?
TravelMate recommends the following safety tips: Always verify your guide's profile and reviews before booking. Share your trip itinerary with a friend or family member. Meet your guide in a public place for the first time. Keep your phone charged and carry emergency contacts. Trust your instincts and report any suspicious behavior to TravelMate support. Do not share personal financial information with guides. Use TravelMate's chat for all trip-related communication.
---
TOPIC: Privacy Policy and Data Protection
TravelMate collects only the information necessary to provide its services, including your name, email, profile details, and booking history. We do not sell your personal data to third parties. Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. You can request to view, update, or delete your personal data at any time by contacting support. Profile photos are stored securely on Cloudinary. Chat messages are stored securely and only visible to the conversation participants.
---
TOPIC: How do I reset my password?
If you forgot your password, click "Forgot Password" on the Sign In page. Enter your registered email address and we will send you a password reset link. Click the link in the email, enter your new password, and confirm it. The reset link expires after 1 hour for security. If you do not receive the email, check your spam folder or contact support.
---
TOPIC: What happens if a guide declines my booking request?
If a guide declines your booking request, you will be notified and can browse other available guides or trip posts. There is no charge for declined booking requests. You can also try contacting the guide through chat to discuss alternative dates or arrangements before sending another request.
---
TOPIC: Can I book multiple guides for the same trip?
Yes, you can send booking requests to multiple guides for different aspects of your trip or different dates. However, you should manage your bookings carefully to avoid scheduling conflicts. Each booking is handled independently and has its own cancellation and payment terms.
---
TOPIC: How do I contact TravelMate support?
If you need help or have a question that is not answered here, you can reach TravelMate support by emailing support@travelmate.com. Our support team typically responds within 24-48 hours. For urgent safety concerns during a trip, please contact local emergency services first and then notify TravelMate.
---
TOPIC: Community Guidelines and Code of Conduct
All TravelMate users must treat each other with respect and professionalism. Discrimination, harassment, hate speech, or threatening behavior is strictly prohibited and will result in immediate account suspension. Guides must provide accurate descriptions of their services and pricing. Travelers must show up on time and respect the guide's expertise. Any disputes should be reported to TravelMate support for mediation.
---
TOPIC: How does TravelMate's AI feature work?
TravelMate uses AI powered by Google Gemini to help users enhance their content. Guides can use AI to improve their profile descriptions and trip post descriptions, making them more engaging and professional. Travelers can enhance their trip request descriptions using AI. The AI takes your raw notes or keywords and transforms them into polished, compelling text. This feature is free and optional.
---
TOPIC: Account Deletion
If you wish to delete your TravelMate account, please contact support at support@travelmate.com with your registered email address. Account deletion is permanent and will remove all your profile data, booking history, reviews, and chat messages. Any pending bookings must be cancelled or completed before account deletion. Account deletion is processed within 7 business days.
---
TOPIC: What notification emails does TravelMate send?
TravelMate sends the following email notifications: a welcome email when you register, booking confirmation emails, booking status updates (accepted, declined, cancelled, completed), and password reset emails. All emails are sent from the official TravelMate email address. You cannot opt out of transactional emails related to bookings and account security.
---
TOPIC: Is TravelMate available worldwide?
TravelMate is a global platform and can be used by travelers and guides from anywhere in the world. However, the availability of guides depends on their location and registration on the platform. We are constantly growing our guide network to cover more destinations. If your preferred destination does not have guides yet, check back regularly or contact us with suggestions.
---
TOPIC: Technical Requirements
TravelMate works on all modern web browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. No app download is required as TravelMate is a fully responsive web application that works on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices. A stable internet connection is required for real-time features like chat and booking. We recommend keeping your browser updated for the best experience.
---
TOPIC: Booking Statuses Explained
Your booking can have the following statuses: PENDING means the guide has not yet responded to your request. ACCEPTED means the guide has confirmed your booking. DECLINED means the guide could not accommodate your request. CANCELLED means either you or the guide cancelled the booking. COMPLETED means the trip has been finished successfully. You can view all your booking statuses in the "My Bookings" section of your dashboard.
